# DeepRoots

DeepRoots is a gamified collaborative productivity platform 
built for students who are tired of studying alone.

Join virtual focus rooms where synchronized Pomodoro timers 
create shared study sessions — turning isolated studying into 
a community-driven experience. Set your tasks, focus together, 
prove your progress, and watch your virtual forest grow with 
every session you complete.

## What makes DeepRoots different?

Most productivity apps track your time. DeepRoots tracks your 
growth. Every session you complete moves you closer to a real 
goal, levels you up, and contributes to a forest that 
represents your entire academic journey.

- **Virtual Focus Rooms** — study with others in real time
- **Synchronized Pomodoro Timer** — everyone on the same countdown
- **AI Task Assistant** — don't know what to study? we got you
- **Focus Forest** — your effort visualized as a growing forest
- **Deep Analytics** — understand how, when and why you study
- **Leveling System** — Bronze to Legend with real rewards
- **Goal Roadmaps** — from your dream to today's to-do list

> Study Together. Focus Deeper. Grow Further.
